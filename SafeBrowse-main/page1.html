Why this is needed ?

In today's digital world, links are everywhere. From marketing emails, social media
posts and messages. However, not all links are safe. Some may lead to phishing
sites, malware downloads or scam pages. Our tool helps protect users against online
threats by analyzing links so users know where they lead.

What We Do?

SafeBrowse is a URL analysis tool to evaluate, analyze and provide detailed
reports about any URL. We provide a comprehensive view of the webpage
by scanning it with various online resources and checking it against malware and
phishing databases. Our tool helps users make informed decisions about
the safety of websites they visit.
